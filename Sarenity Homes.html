Serenity Homes

Serenity Homes, developed by Khwaja Developers (P) Ltd., is an exceptional gated community located along the outer ring road, seamlessly connecting Kanpur Road and Mohan Road in Lucknow. This thoughtfully planned project offers a wide array of residential options, including beautifully designed row houses in various sizes, each with flexible payment schedules to cater to diverse buyer needs. Moreover, Serenity Homes provides an affordable range of plots, giving residents the opportunity to craft their dream homes according to their preferences and aspirations, thereby fostering a strong sense of ownership and belonging.

Residents of Serenity Homes are treated to an array of modern amenities, contributing to a comfortable and enriched living experience. The project boasts efficient drainage systems to ensure proper water management, lush parks, and well-designed play areas for recreation and relaxation. With 30-25 ft. wide roads and strategically placed electricity poles, mobility and convenience are seamlessly integrated into daily life.

Moreover, its prime location adds to its allure, with quick access to major landmarks such as Amausi Station, T.S.M Hospital Engineering College, Navodaya Inter College, and the Lucknow-Agra Expressway, further enhancing its value as an ideal residential and investment destination.
